---
title: "Webhooks"
description: "Get notified when publications and orders change state."
---

<Note>
  Webhooks are on the roadmap. Until they ship, poll
  `GET /publications/{id}/status` or `GET /orders/{id}/status` — both
  endpoints are cheap and designed for polling.
</Note>

## Polling pattern

```bash
while true; do
  curl -s https://insta-buddy-dash.lovable.app/api/public/v1/publications/$ID/status \
    -H "Authorization: Bearer $LOOMLY_API_KEY" \
    | jq -r '.data.status'
  sleep 5
done
```

Stop polling once status is `published`, `failed` or `cancelled`.
